RETURN POLICY

What is your policy on returns?

We understand that you may occasionally wish to return your purchases. We'll be happy to help you with the process.

Except Bedsheets, Curtains, Cushions, Dhurries, Rugs, Polyester Door mats, Table Linen, and Kitchen linen, other household products are Non-Returnable.

We do not accept the return of any furniture item/s that have been delivered.

Please return your household products in their original packaging, within 14 days of delivery.

Double-check that you've included all the tags, accessories, promotional gifts, and warranty cards that came with your order.

What is your policy on Cancellation?

You can cancel your order or a part of your order online on HomeCentre.com as long as you do so before it has been processed.

If it has already been dispatched, you can wait for it to arrive and then refuse the delivery.

Please note, you can also cancel products you’ve bought during a sale.

Once you’ve cancelled an order, your refund will be credited to your card, bank account or wallet after we’ve finished processing the cancellation.
